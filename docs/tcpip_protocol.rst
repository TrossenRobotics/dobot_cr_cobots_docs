===============
TCP/IP Protocol
===============

Dobot robots' primary way of communicating with other platforms is through their TCP/IP protocol.
Full and up-to-date documentation of the protocol can be found at `Dobot-Arm/TCP-IP-Protocol`_ on
GitHub.
